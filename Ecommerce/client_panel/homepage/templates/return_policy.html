Return Policy

Introduction

At DM, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help. Please review our return policy below to understand how to initiate a return or exchange.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Time Frame: You have [Number of Days] days from the date of delivery to return eligible items.
  • Condition of Items: To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in the same condition that you received them, and in their original packaging. Items that show signs of wear or have been altered, washed, or damaged in any way are not eligible for return.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Certain items are final sale and cannot be returned. These include:
    • Gift cards
    • Downloadable software or digital products
    • Personalized or custom-made items
    • Perishable goods (e.g., food, flowers)

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact Us: To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at [Your Contact Information] with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with a return authorization and instructions on how to proceed.
  2. Packaging: Please securely pack the item(s) in the original packaging, including any accessories, tags, and documentation.
  3. Shipping: You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

Refunds

  • Processing: Once we receive and inspect your returned item(s), we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original method of payment within [Number of Days].
  • Partial Refunds: In certain situations, only partial refunds may be granted (e.g., items not in their original condition, damaged items, or items returned more than [Number of Days] after delivery).
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable. If you received free shipping on your order, the cost of shipping will be deducted from your refund.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item for the same product, please contact us at [Your Contact Information] and follow the return process. Once the original item is received and inspected, we will send out the replacement.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet:

  1. Check Your Bank Account: It may take some time before your refund is officially posted.
  2. Contact Your Credit Card Company: Processing time before a refund is posted can vary.
  3. Contact Your Bank: There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.
If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund, please contact us at [Your Contact Information].

Return Policy for International Orders

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs, including any customs duties or taxes. The process for returns and refunds for international orders may vary depending on the country of origin. Please contact our customer service team for specific instructions.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive an item that is damaged or defective, please contact us within [Number of Days] of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. Please review the product description carefully before making a purchase.
